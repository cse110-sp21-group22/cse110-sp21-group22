What is Rose and Thorn?

×
"Rose and thorn" is a fun way to reflect on our daily lives, and can help give us a better mental state of mind. Start by writing "a rose", which can be a highlight, success, or small win of the day. Then write "a thorn", which can be a challenge, or obstacle that you can maybe improve on. Reflect on these, as your entries will refresh daily.
